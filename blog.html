5 Essential Bike Maintenance Tips for a Smooth Ride

Keep your bike in top shape with these simple yet effective maintenance tips.

26 Feb 2024

Vishnu Dev

Keeping your bike in top shape ensures a smoother ride, extends its lifespan, and enhances your safety on the road. Follow these five essential bike maintenance tips to keep your ride in perfect condition.

1. Keep Your Bike Clean

A clean bike is a happy bike! Dirt, mud, and grime can build up over time, affecting performance and causing premature wear and tear.

  1. Use a mild detergent and water to clean your bike.
  2. Pay special attention to the chain, gears, and brake areas.
